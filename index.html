Points Classification — Green Jersey

After Stage 2

Isaac Del Toro (UAE Team Emirates-XRG) leads on 30 points after winning Stage 2 in Barcelona, just ahead of teammate Tadej Pogačar and breakaway rider Alex Molenaar, who topped the intermediate sprint. Sprinters Jasper Philipsen, Olav Kooij and Biniam Girmay are expected to contest the classification once the flat stages arrive later in week one.

Mountains Classification — Polka Dot Jersey

After Stage 2

Alex Molenaar (Caja Rural-Seguros RGA) took the lead after scoring on the Côte de Begues in the Stage 2 breakaway. Tadej Pogačar held the jersey after Stage 1 by topping the Montjuïc climb, and is expected to challenge again once the race reaches the Pyrenees on Stage 3.

Best Young Rider — White Jersey

After Stage 2

Isaac Del Toro (UAE Team Emirates-XRG, born 2003) leads as the highest-placed rider under the age classification's cutoff, sitting 4th overall. Juan Ayuso (Lidl-Trek) and 19-year-old Paul Seixas (Decathlon CMA CGM) are the closest challengers.
